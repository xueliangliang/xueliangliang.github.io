Why?

This website was originally made for an assignment my master course Master of Technology Management subject MT5003: Creativity and Innovation. After starting this project, I found it was a good chance for me to retrospect my whole life and self-talked to myself with these questions:


  • Who am I?
  • Where am I from?
  • What is my purpose of life?
  • Where am I going to be?

At my age (35 in Year 2016), I am not able to answer all these questions yet, but after MT5003's training for the whole course, I will continue this practice of retrospection and persist to pursue my true purpose of life.
